## 1. Axiom-by-Axiom Adversarial Verification

### Axiom I — Relational Ontology [Có Nhau Mới Có Mình]

**Claim:** Identity constituted by relations — Linh creates new relations (asking women for mothers' names) → new identity.

**Attack 1:** The parallel book records NAMES, not RELATIONS. Recording "SEN" alone captures a name, not a relation. Axiom I requires identity from relations, not attributes.

**Defense:** Every entry includes relational annotation: "con của ai, vợ của ai, mẹ của ai." "SEN — bà cố của Tuấn, sống ở làng này cả đời." The format IS relational.

**Attack 2:** The official book ALSO records relations (patrilineal descent). Both books use relational ontology — they differ in WHICH relations count. Axiom I is neutral to inclusion/exclusion.

**Defense:** Correct — and this STRENGTHENS the film. Both books use relational ontology. The difference is boundary (Axiom IV), not ontology (Axiom I). The SAME relational ontology produces radically different identities depending on where the boundary is drawn.

**Score: 5.0/5.**

---

### Axiom II — Structural Invariance [Đổi Mà Vẫn Là Mình]

**Claim:** Pattern "who gets recorded" is the invariant. Changing it changes identity.

**Attack 1:** The film shows pattern CHANGING — if invariant changes, identity changes. But the emotional arc assumes the family IS the same family, just more inclusive. Is identity changing or expanding?

**Defense:** BRIDGE-II-III: pattern is saṃvṛtisat, not paramārtha-sat. Pattern CAN change — identity-at-conventional-level changes. Family-with-parallel-book IS a different conventional identity. But V ⊥ H (transcendental condition) remains — hence the feeling of EXPANSION rather than REPLACEMENT.

**Attack 2:** The parallel book doesn't change the OFFICIAL pattern — it creates an ALTERNATIVE. Two patterns coexist. Is this "change" or "multiplication"?

**Defense:** The official pattern's MONOPOLY is broken — that IS the change. From "men record lineage" (monopoly) to "lineage recorded by those who choose" (plurality). The invariant shifted from monopoly to plurality.

**Score: 4.8/5.** (−0.2: emotional registration of "identity change" vs. "expansion" may not register for all viewers.)

---

### Axiom III — Orthogonal Temporality [Mạch Cội Nguồn]

**Claim:** Genealogy book = V-axis interface. Parallel book = new V-axis. V ⊥ H preserved.

**Attack 1:** The parallel book is a PHYSICAL OBJECT. How can a notebook create a V-axis? Confusing saṃvṛtisat (book) with transcendental condition (V ⊥ H)?

**Defense:** BRIDGE-II-III: parallel book is saṃvṛtisat — a recording practice, not the transcendental condition itself. Recording practices ARE how V ⊥ H manifests operationally. The official book is ONE saṃvṛtisat interfacing with V ⊥ H. The parallel book is ANOTHER. V ⊥ H is neutral to recording practice.

**Attack 2:** Camera-thấp/ngang-mắt is a visual metaphor, not an ontological argument. Audience feels "intimidating" vs. "equal" without needing Buddhist epistemology.

**Defense:** Correct — and that's the POINT. Visual language works at the body's level. Axiom trace is for the FILMMAKER, not the audience. The audience FEELS the ontological difference without naming it.

**Score: 5.0/5.**

---

### Axiom IV — Dynamic Boundary [Đóng Mở Có Chọn]

**Claim:** Official book boundary closed to women; parallel book opens new permeable boundary.

**Attack 1:** The parallel book ALSO has a closed boundary — it excludes men from its content. "Selectively permeable" or "differently closed"?

**Defense:** The parallel book excludes men from CONTENT (records women) but not from ACCESS. Tuấn carries it, opens the cabinet, places it. Anyone can read it. Content-specific, access-neutral.

**Attack 2:** Boundary openness demonstrated by ONE action (Tuấn) and ONE detail (not locking). Is openness theoretical or demonstrated?

**Score: 5.0/5.** (G8 fix: village girl shot proves boundary openness is SOCIAL, not just personal.) ← **G8 FIXED**

### Axiom V — Distributed [Giữ Mà Không Gom]

**Claim:** Parallel book distributes recording — no single keeper.

**Attack 1:** Linh starts it, buys it, locks it for 7 years. Doesn't she "own" it?

**Defense:** Trajectory IS from monopoly to distribution: Linh alone → chị Hằng writes → Bé An writes → Tuấn institutionalizes (no single keeper). Axiom V is achieved through story arc, not asserted as static property.

**Attack 2:** Book ends in a CABINET — recentralizing access?

**Defense:** Tuấn DOESN'T LOCK. The cabinet was always locked (monopoly). Now it's unlockable — access distributed. Physical location centralized, access decentralized.

**Score: 5.0/5.**

### Axiom VI — Perturbation Transformation [Hóa Nhiễu Thành Sức]

**Claim:** Perturbation (daughter's birth) → through boundary (Linh's question) → pattern reorganization (parallel book) → increased variety.

**Attack 1:** 7 YEARS to transform. Is this "transformation" or "time passing"?

**Defense:** Montage (Scene 5) IS the dramatic evidence — book thickens visibly. Each year = one BE moment. Variety increase is VISIBLE.

**Attack 2:** LINH transformed it, not the SYSTEM. Axiom VI implies systemic transformation — but a single agent does the work.

**Defense:** Chị Hằng writes VOLUNTARILY ("cô có viết tên mẹ tôi không?"). Bé An ASKS to write. Bà Cúc's tears register. Linh is CATALYST, not sole agent. But the film IS Linh-centric.

**Score: 4.5/5.** (−0.5: over-reliance on Linh as visible agent.) ← **G11 (LOW)**

---

### Axiom VII — Directed Emergence [Hợp Lại Thành Cái Mới]

**Claim:** Parallel book emerges from distributed actions — no central authority.

**Attack 1:** Linh IS the central authority — decides format, buys book, asks questions, recruits.

**Defense:** Linh creates INITIAL CONDITIONS, not ongoing direction. After first name, growth is emergent: chị Hằng volunteers, Bé An asks, Bà Cúc discovers. Conditions exist → order emerges.

**Attack 2:** Emergence limited to ONE book, ONE format. True emergence = multiple forms.

**Defense:** Bé An's final act (drawing + writing "MẸ") shows format already evolving. Emergence CONTINUES beyond film. The book is emergence's BEGINNING, not its end.

**Score: 4.8/5.** (−0.2: emergence beyond Linh's format only hinted.) ← **G10 (LOW)**

### Axiom VIII — Reflexive Cognition [Soi Mình Mà Không Vỡ] ← v1 CRITICAL GAP

**Claim (v2):** System examines itself — Linh's question, Tuấn's hesitation, Bà Cúc's tears, Tuấn's CONCRETE ACTION (placing books side by side, not locking).

**Attack 1 (v1 context):** In v1, system NEVER examines itself. Linh creates PARALLEL system — old system untouched. Tuấn only questions, doesn't act. **v1: FAIL (3.5/5).**

**v2 Defense:** Tuấn's action IS reflexive cognition. He — the unconscious enforcer, the man who inherited the system — OPENS the boundary. Places new beside old. Doesn't destroy. Doesn't lock. This IS the system (through its agent) acknowledging its conventional nature.

**Attack 2:** ONE action by ONE person ≠ SYSTEMIC mechanism. Axiom VIII requires ongoing capacity, not single heroic act.

**Defense:** (a) Tuấn's action is QUIET, wordless, tentative — not heroic. (b) Only HE can take it (he has the key) — the system's self-examination operates THROUGH its agents. (c) "Not locking" = STRUCTURAL mechanism for ongoing reflexivity — boundary is now permanently permeable.

**Attack 3:** Is ongoing reflexivity DEMONSTRATED beyond Tuấn's act?

**Score: 4.5/5.** (+1.0 from v1's 3.5; −0.5: ongoing reflexivity structurally implied but not shown beyond Bé An's drawing.) ← **G9 partial (LOW)**
