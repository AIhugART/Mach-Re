## ABC Compass Triangulation — Ubuntu in the MACH RE Lens

> **Provenance rule:** The triangulation analysis below is `[ES-ANUMA]` `[proposed-by-this-project]`.

| Compass | How Ubuntu is viewed | Contribution to V⊥H | Gap / Limitation |
|---------|---------------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| **A (Phan Ngọc)** | Ubuntu's ukama = structural extraction of relational patterns from African cultural material. A's method: extract structural invariants from empirical data. Ubuntu's sasa/zamani = two structural invariants operating in African culture. A's vượt gộp (transcend-and-incorporate) maps to Ubuntu community incorporating ancestors without eliminating present relations. | A confirms structural extraction METHOD works cross-culturally. Validates the extraction approach, not the specific V⊥H claim. | A doesn't directly address Ubuntu; mapping is [ES-ANUMA]. |
| **B (Ashby/Weick)** | Ubuntu community = viable system (Ashby): maintains identity across generational turnover (V) while adapting present-moment configurations (H). Weick sensemaking: "I am because we are" = identity constructed through retrospective (zamani/V) + prospective (sasa/H) enactment simultaneously. | B's viable system REQUIRES V and H to be operationally independent: if H change forced V change, the system would lose transgenerational identity. Ubuntu community's survival as a system confirms B's structural requirement. | B is formal/cybernetic; Ubuntu is philosophical/ontological. Lens confirms structural requirement, doesn't prove V⊥H mathematically. |
| **C (Buddhist Epistemology)** | Pratītyasamutpāda = H-axis dependent origination (present-moment causation). Anattā = relational self. BUT: C does NOT have an ancestor-ontological dimension comparable to zamani. C models H-axis excellently but has no equivalent to V-axis ancestor presence. | **CRITICAL INSIGHT (negative evidence):** Because C can model H but NOT V — and Ubuntu has both sasa and zamani — this demonstrates V and H are genuinely distinct dimensions that cannot be reduced to each other. If V were just a form of H, C would have captured it. C's gap IS the evidence. | C's absence of zamani-equivalent is the most important datum: V-axis is not derivable from present-moment relational ontology alone. Strongest argument yet that V and H are genuinely orthogonal. |
