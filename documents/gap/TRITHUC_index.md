## Conventions

- TRITHUC labels: TRITHUC-1 to TRITHUC-N.
- Node codes: N_PN_XXXXX (A), N_AW_ASH_XXXXX / N_AW_WEI_XXXXX (B), N_BE_XXXXX (C).
- Triangulation gap: concept present in ≥1 compass system but weak/absent in ≥2 others.
- Gap type: Structural (formal concept missing), Cultural (empirical case missing), Formal (logical structure missing), Register (analytical level mismatch).
- RCA levels: Strong, Medium, Weak.
- Dash (—) = no corresponding concept in that compass system.
- Mapping rows reference `a_b_c_system_mapping.md` Part II.

- Ý thức pháp luật
- Xây dựng niềm tin công dân (civic trust)

**Exclusion reason:** *Institutional design* register (prescriptive) vs. MACH RE's *decentralized emergent mechanism* register (descriptive). Per `axiom_spec.md §0.1.1`.
